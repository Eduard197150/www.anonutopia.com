About Anote

Anote is a Waves based token, similar to PI, but with few major differences:

  it's a Waves token so it's fully functional right away - you can send it, receive it, exchange it etc.
  it's mined / minted by subscribing to our Telegram ad channels and that's what gives it value right away
  it uses Telegram and Waves wallet which are both mature projects so you can be sure nobody is spying

It will be expanded with new ways to spend it, it's not just mining and marketing, it's also an educational token with a mission to educate people how to use crypto (send, receive, exchange, ...) and to show them projects like Neutrino in order to democratize monetary systems and take monopoly away from them (banks). It's designed to bring crypto to ordinary people.

It is also a main currency of Anonutopia, smart contract country.
