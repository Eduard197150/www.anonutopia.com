Facebook Quest

You can get a reward for sharing a Facebook status about Anonutopia and Anote. The reward is 10 anotes and first time, you will also get 0.00000001 AINTs.

Keep in mind that 0.00000001 AINTs will be a required amount for all withdrawals in the future.

Howto

To claim your reward, follow these simple steps:

  Send /ref command to AnoteRobot
  Copy your referral message
  Publish that referral message in your Facebook status
  Copy link to that Facebook status (time / date bellow your name is a link to your post)
  Send /facebook command to AnoteRobot
  Paste your Facebook post link and you're done
