About Anote

Anote is a Waves based token, similar to PI, but with few major differences:

  it's a Waves token so it's fully functional right away - you can send it, receive it, exchange it etc.
  it's mined / minted by subscribing to our Telegram ad channels and that's what gives it value right away
  it uses Telegram and Waves wallet which are both mature projects so you can be sure nobody is spying

It will be expanded with new ways to spend it, it's not just mining and marketing, it's also an educational token with a mission to educate people how to use crypto (send, receive, exchange, ...) and to show them projects like Neutrino in order to democratize monetary systems and take monopoly away from them (banks). It's designed to bring crypto to ordinary people.

It is also a main currency of Anonutopia, smart contract country.


How to start with Anote?

In order to mine / mint, send, receive and sell / exchange Anote, you need these two applications:

  Telegram Instant Messenger
  Waves Wallet

Once you have those two installed either or your mobile phone or on your computer, simply open one of our Telegram groups - Anonutopia or AnonutopiaBalkan (depending on your location and language) and our friendly Telegram bot AnonsRobot will lead you through the rest of the process. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to ask in one of our Telegram groups.

Anote Referral System

Anote has a referral system similar to one PI uses. It's used to build web of trust, but you can also earn with it. Every time one of your referred users activates a free Anote, you'll receive 0.5. Also, if they start mining, your mining power will increse for 0.05 Anote / hour. So, for example, if you have 5 referred users, your mining power will be 0.45 Anote / hour (0.2 for your basic mining and 5 * 0.05 for referrals).

There are two way to refer somebody to Anote / Anonutopia:

  add them to one of our groups - Anonutopia or AnonutopiaBalkan
  send them your referral link which you can get if you enter /status command using AnonsRobot

If one of your referred users purchases Anote for Waves (creates new denominations), you get 20%.
