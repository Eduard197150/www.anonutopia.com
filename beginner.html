Absolute Beginner's Tutorial

Follow these steps to get started as soon as possible:

  Download and install Telegram Instant Messenger
  Open AnonsRobot Telegram chat bot and click start button
  Subscribe to AnonShout Telegram channel
  Type and send /mine to AnonsRobot
  Copy daily mining code from AnonShout to AnonsRobot
  Join Anonutopia or AnonutopiaBalkan group based on your language preferences

That's it, you're done, after this just follow the instructions you get from AnonsRobot to continue mining.
